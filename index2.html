SnapCarousel

Privacy Policy

Effective: April 8, 2026

SnapCarousel does not collect, store, or share any personal data. Your photos and projects stay on your device.

1. Overview

SnapCarousel is a photo carousel and collage editor for iOS. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our app. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

SnapCarousel does not collect: personal identifiers, location data, photos or media files, usage analytics, device identifiers, financial information, or contacts.

3. Data Stored on Your Device

All projects, photos, and settings are stored locally on your device only. We have no access to this data.

4. Photo Library Access

SnapCarousel requests access to your photo library only when you choose to add a photo to a project. Photos are never uploaded to any server. You can revoke access anytime in Settings.

5. Camera Access

Camera access is requested only when you capture a photo. Captured photos remain on your device and are not transmitted anywhere.

6. Third-Party Services

SnapCarousel does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking frameworks.

7. In-App Purchases

All payment processing is handled entirely by Apple via the App Store. We do not receive or store any payment information.

8. Children's Privacy

SnapCarousel does not collect any information from anyone, including children under 13. Our app is safe for all ages.

9. Your Rights

To remove all app data, simply delete SnapCarousel from your device.

10. Changes to This Policy

If we update this Privacy Policy, we will post the revised version at this URL with a new effective date.
